About Corgis

Corgis are a breed of dog known for their short legs and long bodies, which make them very distinctive and adorable. They are active and intelligent dogs that often have a strong affinity for humans and make great companions.

Fun Facts About Corgis

Corgis were originally bred to herd cattle and sheep.

They are one of the favorite breeds of the British royal family.

Corgis have a strong association with folklore and are said to be the preferred steed of fairy warriors.
